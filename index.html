COMMON CONCERNS

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to send a physical product?

No physical product is needed. Just send your existing product images, raw phone snaps, key product details (features, ingredients, or USP), brand colors, and copy preferences. Our advanced visual AI pipelines handles rendering professional 30-second studio ad layouts from high-resolution layers.

How long does delivery take?

Single test ads and our standard Starter pack ads are delivered securely to your inbox within 48 hours. Enterprise and monthly recurring packages (batching 20 ads) are prepared, curated, and delivered within 7 working days.

Can I use these ads for paid campaigns?

Absolutely yes. All visual creative deliverables are accompanied by full commercial usage rights in perpetuity. You are fully authorized to publish resources across Instagram Ads, TikTok Spark Ads, Facebook, YouTube Shorts, Google Performance Max campaigns, or on your brand website.

What if I'm not happy with the ad?

Every deliverable features 1 free complete revision cycle. You can explicitly share feedback about styling accents, texts, pacing, or presentation, and we will update and render a fresh output within 24 hours.

What niches do you work with?

While our primary specialization is in High-End Beauty, Organic Skincare, Hydrating Cosmetics, and Botanical Oils, we successfully produce ads for closely associated wellness products, natural supplements, active lifestyle assets, and retail brands.

How do I pay?

We securely clear transactions through global systems such as Wise, standard Wire Transfers, and Stripe invoices. All payments are verified securely upfront, with subscription batches cleared on the 1st of each calendar month.
